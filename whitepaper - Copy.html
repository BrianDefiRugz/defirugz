DeFi Rugz Whitepaper

Our Mission

DeFi Rugz is a decentralized protocol dedicated to eliminating scams in the DeFi ecosystem. We aim to build a transparent, community-driven layer of trust using staking, vouching, DAO-based slashing, and NFT-based reputation scoring.

Token Utility ($RUGZ)

$RUGZ is the core utility token powering the ecosystem. It is used for staking, governance, slashing decisions, bounty rewards, and minting soulbound Reputation NFTs.

Staking & Vouching

Users can stake $RUGZ to vouch for new projects. If a vouched project rugs, the stakerâ€™s tokens are slashed. This ensures only trustworthy actors are promoted, creating accountability in the ecosystem.

DAO Governance

The DAO governs the protocol. It votes on slashing decisions, bounty payouts, and treasury allocation. Only active $RUGZ stakers can vote.

Reputation NFTs

Users earn soulbound NFTs that represent their trust level in the ecosystem. These are earned through actions like accurate reporting, vouching, and slashing participation.

Bounty Board

Community members can earn $RUGZ by identifying scams and submitting evidence. Validated bounties are rewarded from the treasury via DAO vote.

Transparency & Treasury

All treasury actions are on-chain and DAO-governed. The treasury is funded by slashing penalties and emission fees.

Launch Plan

We are launching exclusively on Base with a fixed 1B $RUGZ supply, decaying emissions, and a capped DAO treasury. Initial contracts will include ERC-20, staking, NFT minting, and DAO governance.
