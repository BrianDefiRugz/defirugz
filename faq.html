Frequently Asked Questions

What is DeFi Rugz?

DeFi Rugz is a decentralized reputation and trust layer built to expose and slash scammers in the crypto space. We use community-powered vouching, DAO-based slashing, and staking incentives to protect Web3 users.

How does slashing work?

If a vouched project rugs or is flagged for shady behavior, the DAO can vote to slash the stakers who vouched for it. This adds accountability and discourages bad actors.

What are Rugz NFTs?

Reputation NFTs are soulbound badges awarded to trustworthy participants who help keep the ecosystem clean. They are non-transferable and prove your value to the community.

What chain is DeFi Rugz on?

We are proudly deployed exclusively on the Base chain for fast, cheap, and secure transactions.

How do I earn RUGZ?

You can earn $RUGZ by staking, flagging scams, contributing to bounty reports, or actively participating in DAO governance.
